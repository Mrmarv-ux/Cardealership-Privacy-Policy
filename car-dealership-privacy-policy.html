01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to our car dealership, including our physical showroom and online presence. We are the data controller responsible for the personal information you share with us. For specific contact details, please refer to Section 13 or any communications you have received from us directly.

Our services include the sale, purchase, and financing of new and pre-owned vehicles, trade-in assessments, after-sales support, and related promotional activities including digital advertising campaigns.

02

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us, fill out a form, visit our showroom, or respond to one of our advertisements, we may collect:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Location information (city or area)
  • Vehicle preferences, budget range, and purchase intent
  • Trade-in vehicle details
  • Financing and identification information (where applicable and required)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide in communications with us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or interact with our digital content, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and device type
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited, time on site, and click behaviour
  • Referring source (e.g. social media ad, search engine)
  • Geographic location data (city-level)

Information from Advertising Platforms

If you interact with our advertisements on platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, or Google, those platforms may share certain data with us (such as that you submitted a lead form or clicked through to our site) in accordance with their own privacy policies and your account settings on those platforms.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your vehicle enquiries and follow up on leads
  • Arrange test drives, vehicle viewings, or sales appointments
  • Provide vehicle recommendations based on your stated preferences
  • Process vehicle purchases, trade-ins, and financing applications
  • Send you relevant promotional offers, new arrivals, and dealership updates (where permitted)
  • Improve our website performance and advertising effectiveness
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
04

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following grounds:

Contract

Processing required to fulfil a vehicle sale, service booking, or financing arrangement you have entered into with us.

Legitimate Interests

Following up on genuine enquiries, improving our services, and running advertising to connect buyers with vehicles.

Legal Obligation

Where we are required by law to retain or process data, such as transaction records for tax or regulatory purposes.

Consent

Where you have explicitly opted in to receive marketing communications or promotional messages from us.

05

Advertising & Lead Generation

We run digital advertising campaigns on platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and Google to reach people who may be interested in purchasing a vehicle. These campaigns may use tools such as:

  • Lead forms — where you submit your name and contact details directly within an ad
  • Retargeting pixels — small tracking codes on our website that allow platforms to show our ads to people who have previously visited our site
  • Lookalike audiences — where platforms identify new potential customers with similar profiles to our existing enquirers
  • Landing pages — dedicated pages linked to specific ad campaigns that may collect your enquiry details

When you submit a lead form or enquiry through any of these channels, your information is received by us and used solely to follow up on your vehicle interest. We do not sell lead data to third parties.

06

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With financing partners or lenders where you have applied for vehicle financing
  • With our marketing and advertising service providers who help us manage campaigns (under confidentiality agreements)
  • With professional advisers such as accountants or legal counsel where necessary
  • Where required by applicable law, a court order, or regulatory authority
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of our dealership, staff, or customers

Any third parties we work with are required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

07

Data Storage & Retention

Your personal data is stored on secure systems. We take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law. Enquiry data from prospective customers who did not complete a purchase is typically retained for a period of up to 12 months, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised. Data related to completed transactions may be retained for longer periods as required by tax and regulatory obligations.

08

Cookies & Tracking

Our website and advertising pages may use cookies and tracking technologies to improve your experience and measure the performance of our campaigns. These may include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with our pages (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising pixels — used by platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram) to track ad performance and enable retargeting

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings or via your preferences on the relevant advertising platform. Disabling certain cookies may limit your experience on our website.

09

Marketing Communications

If you have enquired about a vehicle or engaged with our dealership, we may contact you with relevant offers, new stock arrivals, or promotional updates. We do this on the basis of legitimate interest or, where required, with your consent.

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying to any message we send, contacting us directly, or using the unsubscribe link in any email. Opting out of marketing does not affect communications related to an active purchase or service appointment.

10

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about you

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations

Object to or restrict certain processing activities

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 13. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

11

Third-Party Websites

Our website or advertisements may contain links to external websites, vehicle listing platforms, or financing portals. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by us directly. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable law. Any revisions will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued engagement with our dealership or website following any such changes constitutes your acknowledgment of the updated policy.

13

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal data, please reach out to us through any of the contact channels provided in your original enquiry, on our website, or at our physical showroom.

We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
